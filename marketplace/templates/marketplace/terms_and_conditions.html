VENDOR AGREEMENT

The Celestial Forge & KIU Stores Ecosystem

1. The OTP Handshake (Payments)

To ensure total security, we use an OTP (One-Time Pin) system. You will only receive payment once you input the buyer's unique 4-digit code into your dashboard. This code acts as proof that the buyer has received and accepted the goods.

2. Pricing & Commission

We believe in "Clean Profits." When you list an item, we automatically add a 10%(under UGX 5000), 8%(under UGX 100,000) and 7%(above UGX 100,000) platform fee and transaction buffer to the final price shown to students. You will receive exactly your asking price in your virtual balance upon successful delivery.

3. Withdrawal Threshold

To keep transaction fees low, earnings are held in your Celestial Balance until they reach UGX 10,000. Once reached, you can initiate a withdrawal to MTN or Airtel Money. A standard network payout fee of UGX 1,000 applies to each withdrawal.

4. Prohibited Items

Selling illegal substances, academic "help" services, or counterfeit goods will result in an immediate and permanent ban of your store and forfeiture of pending balances.
