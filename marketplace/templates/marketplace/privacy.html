Privacy Policy & Terms

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to KIU Stores, operated by Celestial Forge. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our platform to buy or sell goods within the KIU community. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection

To provide a seamless marketplace experience, we collect the following:

  • Account Data: Name, KIU email, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Business Profiles: For vendors, we store store names, bios, and location details (e.g., Hostel/Room numbers).
  • Transaction Data: Details of products bought/sold, delivery status, and payment confirmations.

3. Payment Processing & Security

Payments on KIU Stores are processed via Flutterwave. We do not store your Mobile Money PINs or full credit card numbers on our local servers. We only receive confirmation of successful transactions to move orders into Escrow status.

4. The Escrow System

To protect both students and vendors, KIU Stores acts as a neutral third party. Funds are held securely until the buyer confirms receipt of the item, at which point the vendor’s share is released to their digital wallet.

5. Cookies

We use functional cookies to keep you logged in and remember your cart items. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about your data or a specific order, please contact the Celestial Forge support team via our official WhatsApp line.
